Process log messages from two files. Log messages look like this: “Apr 09 06:08:03.100 INFO detcore: [detcore, dtid 2] finish syscall: close(2) = Ok(0)”

With some complexities:

  • Some entries are multi-line (contain newlines).
  • Some stripping of nondeterministic information is needed for direct comparability.
  • Certain lines are intended to be deterministic/comparable, in their contents, and others in their presence but not their details.
